Tech Empires Privacy Policy

Effective May 5, 2026

What we collect

Tech Empires collects the following data from your device for the sole purpose of showing relevant advertising:

  • Advertising identifier (IDFA on iOS, GAID on Android) — only after you explicitly accept the App Tracking Transparency prompt.
  • Coarse and precise location — only after you grant location permission.
  • Device identifiers and diagnostic data — model, OS version, language, timezone, IP address.

We do not collect your name, email address, contacts, photos, microphone audio, or any account credentials. The game has no accounts and no online play.

Who we share with

The data above is sent to Nuviad (rtb.nuviad.com) and Google AdMob for ad bidding and serving. Their privacy policies:

  • Nuviad — https://nuviad.com/privacy
  • Google AdMob — https://policies.google.com/privacy

Your choices

  • You can deny App Tracking permission at any time via iOS Settings → Tech Empires → Allow Tracking.
  • You can deny location permission at any time via iOS Settings → Tech Empires → Location.
  • Denying either does not disable ads — it only reduces their relevance.

Data retention

Tech Empires itself stores no personal data. Game progress is saved locally on your device only. Uninstalling the app removes everything.
