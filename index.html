How to play

  • Tap NEW MATCH on the main menu, pick a country, then START GAME.
  • Drag with two fingers to pan the map. Pinch to zoom.
  • Tap a unit to select. Drag onto a target (rival, crystal, friendly building) to give an order.
  • Open the BUILD tab on the right to place structures. Start with a Power Plant + Data Center.
  • Use the TRAIN tab to queue units. Interns harvest, Engineers fight, Tech Support repairs.
  • Take down the rival HQ to clear the level.

The game also includes a built-in walkthrough — tap HOW TO PLAY on the main menu anytime to replay it.
