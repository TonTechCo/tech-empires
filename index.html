Features

⚡

Compute & Power

Two resources, one economy. Interns mine data crystals for compute. Power Plants keep the grid online — go negative and your defenses lose damage, your factories slow.

🌍

Six Nations

USA, China, Germany, Israel, France, Russia. Each with combat and economy quirks. Voice-overs speak in your country's accent. Rival nations are rolled per level.

⚔️

Real-Time Combat

Drag-select your team, give orders, watch the AI counter-press. Defenses auto-fire on threats. Drones fly over walls. AI Agents and Hackers unlock through tech research.

📚

Built-In Tutorial

A guided walkthrough teaches every gesture and mechanic on your first match. Tap HOW TO PLAY anytime to replay it. Offline play, no account, auto-save every 30 seconds.

How to Play

  1. Tap NEW MATCH, pick a country, then START GAME.
  2. Drag with two fingers to pan the map. Pinch with two fingers to zoom.
  3. Open BUILD on the right to place a Power Plant and Data Center. Mine compute, stay positive on power.
  4. Open TRAIN to queue units. Interns harvest, Engineers fight, Tech Support repairs and heals.
  5. Tap a unit to select. Drag onto a target — ground (move), rival (engage), crystal (harvest), friendly building (repair).
  6. Take down the rival HQ to clear the level.

Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 5, 2026

Data We Collect

  • Advertising Identifier (IDFA on iOS, GAID on Android): Collected only after you accept Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt. Used for ad personalization. You can opt out at any time in Settings.
  • Coarse and precise location: Collected only after you grant location permission. Used to serve relevant ads. Not stored on our servers.
  • Device diagnostics: Model, OS version, language, timezone, and IP address — sent with each ad request to bidders.

Third-Party Services

Tech Empires uses two ad networks for monetization:

  • Nuviad — bid request endpoint https://nuviad.com/privacy
  • Google AdMob — fallback layer https://policies.google.com/privacy

Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not require user accounts or registration
  • We do not collect names, emails, contacts, photos, or microphone audio
  • We do not have multiplayer or any online play
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites beyond the ad networks listed above

Your Choices

  • Deny App Tracking permission via iOS Settings → Tech Empires → Allow Tracking. The game keeps working; ads become less relevant.
  • Deny location permission via iOS Settings → Tech Empires → Location. Same story — ads still serve, just generically.

Data Retention

Tech Empires itself stores no personal data on remote servers. Game progress is saved locally on your device only. Uninstalling the app removes everything.

Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
